Instructions

- To move the player around drag the mouse in the direction you want it to go in.

- Hitting the back of another player will result in the other player losing 1 health point.

- Land on a launch pad to increase the players speed.

- Once a launch pad is touched and used, the launch pad will be disabled for a length of time.

- All avatars will have a limited range of motion while moving and all avatars will have the same turning speed such that a player cannot rapidly change directions apart from using a launch pad.

- Hitting the wall at a fast speed will result in player losing control of their character for a time.
